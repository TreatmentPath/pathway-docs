Domain Setup Guide

Learn how to configure your custom domain for sending emails through Pathway. This guide covers everything from adding your domain to configuring DNS records for optimal deliverability.

Before you begin

You'll need access to your domain's DNS settings. This is typically managed through your domain registrar (like GoDaddy, Namecheap) or DNS provider (like Cloudflare, Route 53).
